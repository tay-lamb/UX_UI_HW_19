NSA UI Redesign

Background:

This was a bootcamp case study where we were tasked with redesigning the UI for a governmental agency. I began the project with a smaller group for the ideation phase then we later branched off to create our own respective designs for desktop and mobile. While the existing UI for the National Security Agency is effective, our team felt the website should serve as a resource for citizens/users. Our goal was to make the website feel visually welcoming while also maintaining the NSA's professional and informative core values.

My Role:

I completed user interviews, card-sorting, textual hierarchy design, button states, wireframes, prototyping, and usability testing.

  • Overall: 4 weeks
  • Discovery & Research: 2 weeks
  • Design & Testing: 2 weeks

Ideation Phase: Heuristics Evaluation
